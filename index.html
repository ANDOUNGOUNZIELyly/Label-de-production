BVM ASSOCIATION

BIG VISION MUSIC is a non-profit association, sponsored by BIG VISION MUSIC Label, whose mission is to support artists in their recording and marketing process and in the development of their career.
It is active in several areas including the production of musical artists, the distribution of their music, the production of videograms and event decoration.

BIG VISION MUSIC


BIG VISION MUSIC is a sub label of BIG VISION MUSIC LABEL Production. Its purpose is to produce artists and promote their musical work up to the communication campaign for their record. BIG VISION MUSIC supports the production and promotion of artists' titles and has the necessary resources to meet their needs both materially and financially.

BIG VISION RECORD


BIG VISION RECORD is the recording studio of Big Vision Music Association. It provides artists with an ultra-professional framework to accompany them in the realization of all their artistic projects, namely: Beat Making (Makossa, Bitskusi, Afro Njang, Rumba, Afro Gospel, Hip-Pop, Afro Beat), voice, audio mixing, mix-mastering...etc

BIG VISION EVENEMENTIEL


BIG VISION EVENEMENTIEL offers specialized services in the design and organization of various festive events such as birthdays, weddings, Gala Evening, Christmas Tree, Product Launch, etc. He takes care of the preparations, the logistical and material organization of events.We take care of the entire organization of your professional events.
